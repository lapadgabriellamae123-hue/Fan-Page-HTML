Vinsmoke Sanji

Vinsmoke Sanji, often referred to as "Black Leg" Sanji, is the cook of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is a master of the "Black Leg Style" of martial arts, which he uses to fight opponents with only his legs. His dream is to find the All Blue, a legendary sea where all four seas and their fish species meet.
